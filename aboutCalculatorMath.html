About

Calculator Math is a calculator app for iOS. The app can run on any iPhone using iOS 8 or greater. This app can perform basic arithmetic while keeping the order of operations; even with negatives. Just be sure to use the negative sign and not the minus sign if you want to say a number is negative.

There is also a history. The app stores up to 30 previous calculations. you can tap on either the expression or the result to paste it into the input box. Download it now on the appstore. Click Here
