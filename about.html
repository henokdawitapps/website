About

Calculator Math is a "calculator app" for mobile devices. The app can run on every "iPhone" using iOS 8 or greater. This calculator app has a cursor. The cursor can be moved using the arrow buttons. The calculator app can store up to 30 previous calculations in it's history. The calculator app can solve all expressions that involve addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, exponents, parentheses, and of course numbers.
